Jake Marvin graduated Summa Cum Laude from Hastings College with a degree in Business Administration and Economics.

He worked in the management program for FirstBank for three years, leading groups from personal banking to treasury management to commercial and industrial lending. He then made a unique career change and went on to play two years of professional basketball in Europe before returning stateside in summer of 2019.

He has experience in many different areas of financial coaching and high school education, and now is working on entrepreneurial endeavors to help educate students and young adults in the field of personal finance and debt management. He wants to use his passion for financial education to give back to students and young adults in any way possible. He has a heart for the next generation and offers a proactive solution to many of the challenges that our youth will face in their upcoming years!

Mission Statement

To empower students and young adults to set themselves up for an advantageous future through Proactive solutions, long-term thinking, and incredible decision-making.
